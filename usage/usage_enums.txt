Usage:

  vdb enums [OPTIONS]

Options:

  -h --help                 Print this info
  -v --verbose              Print as much as possible (debugging)
  -w --warnings             Show warnings (suppressed by default)
  -E --errors-off           Do not show any error messages

  All enumerations get printed to stdout in the following form (CSV):
  
    'ENUM_NAME,ORDINAL_VALUE,ORDINAL_NAME'
  
Examples:

  Shows just the PDU_FAMILY enumeration values: 
  
    $ vdb enums | grep PDU_FAMILY
